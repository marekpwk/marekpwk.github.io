Pair programming

Before my first pairing session I was really stressed, never having paired with anyone , I had never solved any programming problem with a real person. I used only resources mainly , asked questions online when I didn't understand something, but I found it was totally different from pair programming.

My first two pairing sessions were really figuring out how this works, like how to divide the responsibilities between navigator and the driver.

I think it was after my third pairing session that I saw the real power of pair programming. Not only did we solve problem quickly, but I also benefited greatly from the knowledge of my colleague. Also, when he was asking questions regarding my way of solving the problem, it allowed me to see it from a different and wider perspective, which helped later in finalizing the solution.

I don't know how many times I have paired since then, it has been a lot, and the only thing I can say, is that I wish I had started this earlier, because these sessions have greatly seeded my learning process.
