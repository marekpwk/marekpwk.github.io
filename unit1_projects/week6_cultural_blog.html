Stereotype

This was the first time I came across “Stereotype threat”, I have never heard of it before and haven't realized the consequences of it. According to Steele's research one of the major barriers holding back the achievement of Blacks, women and other underrepresented groups is the threat of being viewed through the lens of a negative stereotype or the fear of doing something that would inadvertently confirm that stereotype.

It is really scary how wrong beliefs can stop people from achieving the goals they want, and also affect how they see their own strengths. I think one of the best ways of fighting it is to publicize research which demystifies them and talk to and inspire people who we feel might be affected by “stereotype threat”
