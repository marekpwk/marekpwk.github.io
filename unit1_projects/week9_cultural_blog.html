Learning

My thinking style oscillates between Abstract Random and Abstract Sequential, so I need a little bit of everything: lecturers, books, group projects. During Phase 0 I found out how beneficial learning pair programming sessions were. When I started my adventure with programming I used a lot of online resources, but never ever learned with someone else.

During my learning process I also found out that struggling with a problem works really good for me, even if I don't solve the exercise, I understand the explanation from someone else much better, so googling the problem(although I think it is a time saver in a developers life) , at this point is the last thing I do.

During my DBC time, I'm planning to use every minute effectively. I will be commuting by train an hour each way, so during that time I plan to read books related to our curriculum, to get a better understanding of material and kill time during travel :)
